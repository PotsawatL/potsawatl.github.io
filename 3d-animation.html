(Short Animation) How I survived an unexpected situation


I thoroughly enjoyed working on this project. It allowed me to experiment and apply my diverse skills to create a 3D model of myself. Additionally, I incorporated principles from my field trips and university studies to bring this 3D animation project to life.

For this project, I worked independently, primarily using Maya. I created my own character model and designed an entire city. I also applied my experience in lighting and coloring to enhance the project. For basic animation tasks, I utilized Maximo.
