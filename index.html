About Me

Hello, My name is Potsawat Lalitlakkanakul, also known as P.
I recently graduated degree in Digital Media Engineering from the International Program at Khon Kaen University. I'm obsessed with the idea of self-improvement and eager for a platform to grow and excel.

I am deeply passionate about game development and have a robust foundation in game design documentation, gameplay mechanics, and programming. My experience spans a variety of genres, including rogue-like, horror, action, simulation, and VR shooting.

I have experience designing and implementing core gameplay mechanics for AA game titles, ensuring engaging and immersive player experiences. My role involved conducting extensive playtesting sessions to refine and perfect gameplay elements. I collaborated closely with narrative teams to seamlessly integrate storylines and character development into the gameplay. Additionally, I developed visually captivating game environments that enhance the overall aesthetic and player immersion.

I'm currently looking for full-time opportunities where I can leverage my skills and continue to grow. Feel free to ask me anything; I'm happy to help if I can.
