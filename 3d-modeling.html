Nid Model

I am grateful to the Thai Animation and Computer Graphic Association (TACGA)
for the opportunity to participate in this project. It significantly enhanced my understanding of the steps and methods involved in model production.

Initially, I had no knowledge of modeling, so I began by seeking out an interesting project. I was fortunate to join this one, where I studied the provided instructions and received positive feedback.

Nid Model - Before

Nid Model - After

The feedback, both before and after, highlights many shortcomings visible in the pictures. However, these also showcase my progress, making me feel very proud and inspired.

This study enhanced my understanding of the production process and allowed me to apply my knowledge to further development. For instance, I used this insight to inform my colleagues about my modeling mistakes for improvement and discussed the principles of anatomy I studied to refine character design. This experience also deepened my appreciation for drawing.


Snow House Model

I also attempted to model houses and props, using sketches from my friend to replicate them as accurately as possible. This experience enhanced my understanding of product creation and provided valuable knowledge for developing my future indie games.
