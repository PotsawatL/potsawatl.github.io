[Demo] Isekai Survival


Develop a 2D game platform following the theme "Isekai" or "another world", shoot and survive from monsters coming from another world in the Forest.

"Isekai Survival" is a 2D game platform which is letting players use their decision-making skills to survive from monsters. The player is free to press attack on the enemy and aim the attack direction of the character itself to make it more fun and challenging.
