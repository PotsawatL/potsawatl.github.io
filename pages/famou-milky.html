Famous Milky - Gameplay Overview


Famous Milky is a game where players need to collect items according to the map as quickly as possible while evading a big muscular man chasing them.

Game Concept

In Famous Milky, you play as a fitness business executive enjoying a TV show in your living room. Suddenly, a bodybuilder invades your space and attacks you. You must quickly find a way to survive and escape from the intruder.

Controls

  • W/A/S/D to move your character
  • Shift to sprint
  • E to collect items
  • F to toggle the flashlight

Additional Notes

My responsibilities for this project included designing the game and levels, debugging code, and developing AI behavior. Ensuring a balanced and engaging gameplay experience was a key focus.
