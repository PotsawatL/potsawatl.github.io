Concept Character Design - Higanbana

This character is a demigod residing in a secluded, abandoned countryside. She serves as the guardian of the Shrine of the Death Gods, awaiting the return of her revered deity. Gifted with the power of the Death God, she has lived for centuries yet retains the appearance and vitality of youth. She likes to wear ancient clothing, and her beautiful figure gives those who see her a sense of mystery and charm.
