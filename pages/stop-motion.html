Stop Motion - Nightmare


I’d like to share a project I completed with my teammates called “Nightmare.” To keep costs low, we used common equipment. The story revolves around a young man who, unable to forget his ex-girlfriend, turns to drugs. This project falls within the horror genre. We used Adobe Premiere Pro for editing and took frame-by-frame photos for the animation. Additionally, we enhanced the clip with various effects and adjusted the lighting and colors to better match the project’s mood and concept.

My Duties:

I initiate projects and plan their workflow, delegating tasks to my teammates. My primary responsibilities include preparing preproduction materials, writing the storyboard, and defining character roles. After completing these main tasks, I assist my team by creating models and props, and editing the video to enhance its quality, as demonstrated in the link I have attached.
