2D Animation

Content

I felt that this was an opportunity for me to understand more about making these animations. I wanted to know about these processes from watching their animations and from my experience playing the games where they had scenes from their games. That’s why I was interested and wanted to try it.

The result was satisfying and made me understand the process more than I expected. Making animation for me is not just making motion pictures, but it is understanding the principles of everyday movement that we can see everywhere. It opened my eyes to new perspectives and made me understand the creation process more than I previously did.

I am very honored that the Thai Animation and Computer Graphic Association (TACGA) gave me the opportunity to join this project. This study helped me understand more about the production process and allowed me to use my knowledge to develop further. It is an honor to present these certificates.
